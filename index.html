Hockey Fights Cancer brings together the hockey community to support friends and family affected by cancer. Each of the 31 NHL Clubs celebrates their own Hockey Fights Cancer Night, each November. Each player wears a special Hockey fights cancer jersey and coaches and broadcasters wear HFC ties. The organization helps raise money and support patients of the American Cancer Society, Canadian Cancer Society, and Movember Foundation.
